## TL;DR
**SPECS** — Standardised Procedure for Evaluating Creative Systems — is a three-step methodology: (1) state what creativity means for the system, (2) derive concrete tests, (3) apply tests. Provides 14 empirically-derived components of creativity drawn from corpus analysis of how researchers describe creativity.

## Key thesis
Creativity evaluation lags creative-system development. Most papers evaluate by ad-hoc human judgment or single metrics. SPECS forces a system to declare which components of creativity it claims and to derive measurements *before* generating outputs. The 14 components emerged from a corpus analysis of how the field actually talks about creativity.

## Key concepts
- **SPECS three steps** — state, derive, apply.
- **14 components** — active involvement & persistence; domain competence; value; originality; intention; thinking & evaluation; general intellect; social interaction; spontaneity; progression; uncertainty; etc.

## Why it matters for ai-ideator
Defensible evaluation rubric. ai-ideator must commit, in writing, to which of Jordanous's 14 components it claims and how each is measured. Without that, evaluation collapses into anecdotal cherry-picking. Particularly relevant for the Phase-4 evaluator: expand each component into a sub-rubric and use heterogeneous judges.

## Memorable passages
