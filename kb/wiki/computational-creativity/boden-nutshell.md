## TL;DR
The cleanest published statement of Boden's three-fold taxonomy. A compressed, accessible restatement of *The Creative Mind*, with the question of whether computers can be creative as the framing device. Combinational creativity is defined as producing unfamiliar but interesting combinations of familiar ideas — exactly what an LLM concept-combiner does.

## Key thesis
The same three-mode framework as Boden 1990, presented as a short essay. The distinguishing move is making *novelty + surprise + value* the operational triple by which any creative output (human or machine) is assessed.

## Key concepts
- **combinational / exploratory / transformational** — the three modes.
- **novelty + surprise + value** — the evaluation triple.

## Why it matters for ai-ideator
The most-citable short-form source for the combinational/exploratory/transformational split. Use as the canonical citation when defining the system's mode and its evaluation criteria.
