## TL;DR
Integrates an LLM into group **brainwriting** (the structured silent variant of brainstorming) for both divergent and convergent stages. Builds an LLM evaluation engine that scores ideas on relevance, innovation, insightfulness; validates against human expert/novice raters.

## Key thesis
LLMs can productively augment group ideation in both divergent (idea-generation) and convergent (selection) phases. Calibrating the LLM-as-judge against human experts is sufficient to drive the convergent stage — full human convergence is not needed.

## Key concepts
- **brainwriting** — structured silent ideation; less domination than open brainstorming.
- **calibrated LLM-as-judge** — judge prompts validated against human raters.

## Why it matters for ai-ideator
Useful template if ai-ideator targets teams. Brainwriting is a strong protocol because it is empirically more productive than open brainstorming for ideation. The judge calibration insight is reusable for any group setting.
