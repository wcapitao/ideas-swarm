## TL;DR
**Bisociation** is Arthur Koestler's term (1964) for the simultaneous activity of two normally separate "matrices of thought" upon a single situation. It is the cognitive mechanism behind humour (incongruity resolved), scientific discovery (deep structure shared), and artistic invention (meaningful juxtaposition). The single most useful concept for an LLM ideation system that combines concepts from a knowledge base.

## Definition
Routine thinking happens within a single matrix — a rule-governed frame whose internal operations are predictable. Bisociation is the moment when one situation activates *two* matrices simultaneously and a coherent meaning emerges that belongs to neither alone.

## Why it is structurally different from mere combination
- *Combination* = additive concatenation of concepts (X + Y → "X and Y").
- *Bisociation* = projection of one situation through two distinct frames (X | Y), producing structure that exists only at the intersection.

The blend may have **emergent structure** (Mill's "mental chemistry"; Fauconnier-Turner's "emergent structure in the blended space"): properties true of the blend that were true of neither input.

## Operational cue for ai-ideator
A retrieved concept-pair is a *candidate bisociation*. The system's job is to:
1. Identify the matrix each concept belongs to.
2. Find the structural overlap (Fauconnier-Turner's "generic space").
3. Project both into a shared blended space.
4. Surface the emergent structure as the candidate idea.
5. Score for novelty, utility, and surprise.

The success rate is heavily a function of **matrix distance**: too close → trivial blend (same matrix in slight disguise); too far → no coherent intersection. The Goldilocks zone is where Koestler's bisociation yields its best work.

## Worked examples (Koestler)
- *Gutenberg* — wine-press matrix + coin-stamp matrix → printing press (movable type pressed onto paper).
- *Archimedes* — bath-volume matrix + crown-purity matrix → density-by-displacement.
- *Kepler* — magnetism matrix + planetary motion matrix → action-at-a-distance gravity-like force.

Each is a structural projection, not a list union.
