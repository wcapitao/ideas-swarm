## TL;DR

Systematic review of Aloe vera's biological properties and clinical evidence, covering wound healing, anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and gastroprotective effects. Notes traditional use for GI complaints and limited but suggestive evidence for mucosal healing in gastritis and ulcers, while highlighting the need for larger clinical trials.

## Key concepts

- **Aloe vera mucilage** — Gel with mucosal-coating and anti-inflammatory properties, traditionally used for GI symptom relief.
- **Gastroprotective potential** — Preliminary evidence for aloe vera in gastric mucosal healing, primarily from in-vitro and small clinical studies.
