## TL;DR

Study investigating the gastroprotective mechanism of ginger (*Zingiber officinale*) rhizome extract, demonstrating inhibition of COX enzymes and suppression of gastric acid secretion. Provides mechanistic evidence for ginger's traditional use in gastrointestinal complaints, linking its anti-ulcer activity to specific biochemical pathways.

## Key concepts

- **Ginger gastroprotection** — *Zingiber officinale* extract protects gastric mucosa through multiple mechanisms including COX inhibition and acid secretion suppression.
- **COX inhibition** — Ginger compounds inhibit cyclooxygenase enzymes involved in inflammation and gastric acid regulation.
