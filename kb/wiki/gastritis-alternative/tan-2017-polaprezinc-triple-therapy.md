## TL;DR

Multicenter RCT showing that adding polaprezinc (zinc-L-carnosine) to standard clarithromycin-based triple therapy improves H. pylori eradication rates and reduces gastric mucosal inflammation more effectively than triple therapy alone. Provides strong clinical evidence for zinc-carnosine as an adjunct to conventional H. pylori treatment.

## Key concepts

- **Triple therapy adjunct** — Adding polaprezinc to standard PPI + clarithromycin + amoxicillin regimen improves outcomes.
- **Mucosal inflammation reduction** — Polaprezinc provides additional anti-inflammatory benefit beyond antibiotic eradication.
