## TL;DR

Systematic review and meta-analysis of ginger's effectiveness for nausea and vomiting, with particular focus on pregnancy and chemotherapy contexts. Concludes that ginger is a safe and effective antiemetic, superior to placebo for pregnancy-related nausea. Relevant to gastritis as nausea is a cardinal symptom and ginger's GI mechanisms overlap with gastroprotection.

## Key concepts

- **Ginger as antiemetic** — Consistent evidence across multiple RCTs supporting ginger for nausea prevention.
- **Safety profile** — Ginger is generally recognized as safe, including during pregnancy, at typical therapeutic doses.
