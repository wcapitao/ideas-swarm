## TL;DR

Popular book arguing that many GI complaints attributed to excess stomach acid are actually caused by insufficient acid (hypochlorhydria). Wright advocates betaine HCl supplementation and challenges the conventional paradigm of acid suppression therapy. Influential in naturopathic and functional medicine circles, though the hypochlorhydria hypothesis remains controversial in mainstream gastroenterology.

## Key concepts

- **Hypochlorhydria** — Insufficient stomach acid production, proposed as an underdiagnosed cause of dyspepsia, GERD, and nutrient malabsorption.
- **Betaine HCl supplementation** — Oral acid supplementation used in naturopathic practice to address suspected hypochlorhydria.
