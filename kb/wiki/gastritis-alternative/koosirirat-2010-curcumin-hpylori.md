## TL;DR

RCT examining curcumin's anti-inflammatory effect in H. pylori-infected patients. Curcumin supplementation reduced markers of gastric inflammation, supporting its potential as an adjunctive therapy in H. pylori-associated gastritis by modulating the inflammatory cytokine response.

## Key concepts

- **Cytokine modulation** — Curcumin reduces pro-inflammatory cytokines in gastric mucosa of H. pylori-infected patients.
- **Anti-inflammatory adjunct** — Potential role for curcumin alongside conventional H. pylori eradication therapy.
