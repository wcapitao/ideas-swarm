## TL;DR

Influential review establishing the concept that gut microbiota can influence brain chemistry and behavior through the microbiota-gut-brain axis. Discusses evidence from germ-free animal models, probiotic interventions, and pathogen studies showing that gut bacteria modulate the HPA stress axis, neurotransmitter systems, and inflammatory signaling. Coined early conceptual groundwork for "psychobiotics."

## Key concepts

- **Microbiota-gut-brain axis** — Extension of the gut-brain axis concept to explicitly include the microbiome as a key signaling node.
- **Psychobiotics** — Probiotics that confer mental health benefits through gut-brain signaling; a term later formalized by the same authors.
- **HPA axis modulation** — Gut microbiota influence the hypothalamic-pituitary-adrenal stress response.
