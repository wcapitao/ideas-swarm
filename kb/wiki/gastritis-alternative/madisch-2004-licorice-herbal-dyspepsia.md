## TL;DR

Randomized controlled trial evaluating a licorice-containing herbal preparation for functional dyspepsia. Results showed significant improvement in dyspeptic symptoms compared to placebo, supporting the use of herbal combinations including licorice root in upper GI symptom management.

## Key concepts

- **Functional dyspepsia** — Chronic upper abdominal discomfort without identifiable structural cause; a common indication for alternative GI therapies.
- **Phytotherapy RCT** — Controlled trial of a multi-herb preparation, providing higher-quality evidence for herbal gastric therapy.
