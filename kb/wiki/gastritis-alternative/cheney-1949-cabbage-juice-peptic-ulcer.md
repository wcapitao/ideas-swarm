## TL;DR

Landmark 1949 case series reporting remarkably rapid healing of peptic ulcers in 13 patients treated with fresh cabbage juice. Average healing time was 10.4 days compared to the then-standard 37 days with conventional therapy. Cheney attributed the effect to an unidentified "anti-peptic ulcer dietary factor" later termed vitamin U (S-methylmethionine).

## Key concepts

- **Cabbage juice therapy** — Fresh raw cabbage juice as a therapeutic intervention for peptic ulcer disease; one of the earliest naturopathic ulcer treatments with documented case data.
- **Vitamin U** — Proposed anti-ulcer factor in cabbage juice, later identified as S-methylmethionine.
