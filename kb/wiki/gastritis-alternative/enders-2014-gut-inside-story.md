## TL;DR

Bestselling popular science book making gut physiology, the microbiome, and digestive health accessible to a general audience. Covers gastric acid production, gut motility, the enteric nervous system, and microbiome composition in an engaging narrative format. While not primary research, the book has significantly influenced public understanding of GI health and driven interest in alternative and integrative approaches to digestive conditions.

## Key concepts

- **Gut physiology popularization** — Accessible explanation of gastric function, motility, and microbiome science for lay audiences.
- **Microbiome awareness** — Contributed to mainstreaming of gut microbiome concepts in public health discourse.
