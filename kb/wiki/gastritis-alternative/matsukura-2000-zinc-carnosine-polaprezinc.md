## TL;DR

Foundational paper on zinc-L-carnosine (polaprezinc), describing its pharmacological properties including gastroprotective, antioxidant, and anti-ulcer effects. The chelated zinc-carnosine complex adheres to ulcer sites and provides sustained zinc release, promoting mucosal healing. Widely prescribed in Japan for gastric ulcer treatment.

## Key concepts

- **Zinc-L-carnosine (polaprezinc)** — Chelate of zinc and L-carnosine that adheres to gastric ulcer sites, providing localized antioxidant and healing effects.
- **Gastroprotection** — Mucosal defense enhancement through antioxidant activity, membrane stabilization, and growth factor stimulation.
