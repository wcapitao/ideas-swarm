## TL;DR

Study providing evidence that duodenogastric bile reflux causes a distinct pattern of chemical (reactive) gastropathy and may contribute to Barrett's esophagus via duodenogastro-oesophageal reflux. Dixon et al. characterize the histological features of bile reflux gastritis as distinct from *H. pylori*-associated inflammation, establishing bile reflux as an independent gastritis etiology.
