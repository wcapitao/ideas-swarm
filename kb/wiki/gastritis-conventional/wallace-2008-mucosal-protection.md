## TL;DR

Comprehensive physiological review of gastric mucosal defense mechanisms — the pre-epithelial mucus-bicarbonate barrier, epithelial tight junctions and restitution, mucosal blood flow, and the central role of prostaglandins in coordinating all of these. Explains why NSAIDs, by inhibiting prostaglandin synthesis, dismantle multiple layers of defense simultaneously and cause gastropathy.
