## TL;DR

First comprehensive culture-independent (16S rRNA) survey of the human gastric microbiome, revealing a diverse community of 128 phylotypes far beyond *H. pylori* alone. Bik et al. overturned the assumption that the stomach is essentially sterile outside of *H. pylori*, opening the field of gastric microbial ecology and its implications for gastritis pathogenesis and gastric carcinogenesis.
