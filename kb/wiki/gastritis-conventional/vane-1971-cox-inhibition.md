## TL;DR

Nobel Prize-winning paper demonstrating that aspirin and related drugs exert their anti-inflammatory, analgesic, and antipyretic effects by inhibiting cyclooxygenase (COX) and thereby blocking prostaglandin synthesis. This discovery is foundational to understanding NSAID gastropathy, since the same prostaglandins are essential for gastric mucosal protection.
