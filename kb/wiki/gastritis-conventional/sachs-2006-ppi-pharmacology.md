## TL;DR

Comprehensive pharmacology review of PPIs covering their acid-dependent activation mechanism, the role of CYP2C19 genetic polymorphisms in metabolism, and the critical synergy between acid suppression and antibiotic efficacy in *H. pylori* eradication regimens. Explains why PPIs are the indispensable backbone of all standard eradication therapies.
