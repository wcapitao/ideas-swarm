## TL;DR

AGA expert review systematically evaluating the risks attributed to long-term PPI use — including kidney disease, fractures, dementia, and microbiome disruption. The review finds that most associations are based on low-quality observational data with modest effect sizes, but recommends periodic reassessment of PPI necessity and use of the lowest effective dose.
