## TL;DR

Seminal 1984 Lancet paper reporting the association of unidentified curved bacilli (later named *Helicobacter pylori*) with active chronic gastritis and peptic ulceration. Marshall and Warren demonstrated the organism in gastric biopsies from 58 of 100 consecutive patients, fundamentally overturning the prevailing stress-and-acid paradigm of gastritis and ulcer disease.
