## TL;DR

MAPS II is the European guideline for surveillance of gastric precancerous conditions — atrophic gastritis, intestinal metaplasia, and dysplasia. It provides evidence-based endoscopic mapping protocols (Sydney system biopsies), OLGA/OLGIM staging for risk stratification, and surveillance interval recommendations. The guideline is the international benchmark for structured follow-up of patients at elevated gastric cancer risk.
