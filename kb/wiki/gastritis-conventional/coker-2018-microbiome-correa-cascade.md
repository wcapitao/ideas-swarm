## TL;DR

Landmark study mapping gastric mucosal microbiome changes across every stage of the Correa cascade — from superficial gastritis through atrophic gastritis, intestinal metaplasia, and gastric cancer. Coker et al. show progressive enrichment of oral-type bacteria and disrupted microbial interaction networks as carcinogenesis advances, providing a microbiome-level framework that parallels the histological cascade.
