## TL;DR

Landmark Japanese prospective cohort study following 1,526 patients for a mean of 7.8 years, demonstrating that gastric cancer developed exclusively in *H. pylori*-infected patients (36 of 1,246 infected vs 0 of 280 uninfected). Uemura et al. established that the pattern and severity of gastritis — particularly severe corpus atrophy and intestinal metaplasia — are the key determinants of cancer risk among infected individuals.
