## TL;DR

Maastricht VI updates the European *H. pylori* consensus with emphasis on vonoprazan-based regimens, precision (susceptibility-guided) therapy, integration of microbiome considerations, and expanded screen-and-treat approaches for gastric cancer prevention in high-risk populations. It reflects the evolution from empiric to personalized eradication strategies.
