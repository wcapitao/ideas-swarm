## TL;DR

Comprehensive review of autoimmune atrophic gastritis covering its immunopathogenesis (anti-parietal cell and anti-intrinsic factor antibodies), the characteristic corpus-restricted pattern of atrophy, consequent enterochromaffin-like (ECL) cell hyperplasia with carcinoid tumor risk, and current management strategies including B12 replacement and endoscopic surveillance.
