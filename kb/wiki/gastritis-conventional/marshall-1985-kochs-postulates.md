## TL;DR

Marshall ingested a culture of *H. pylori* to fulfil Koch's postulates, developing acute gastritis confirmed by biopsy. This self-experiment provided the critical causal link between the bacterium and gastric disease, moving the field from association to causation and ultimately leading to the 2005 Nobel Prize.
