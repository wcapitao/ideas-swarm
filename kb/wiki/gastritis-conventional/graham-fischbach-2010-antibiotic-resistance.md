## TL;DR

Review documenting the global rise of *H. pylori* antibiotic resistance — particularly to clarithromycin and metronidazole — and its impact on eradication rates. Graham and Fischbach argue that the era of empiric triple therapy is ending and that susceptibility testing or local resistance surveillance must guide regimen selection.
