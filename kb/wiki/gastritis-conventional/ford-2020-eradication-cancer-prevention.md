## TL;DR

Meta-analysis of RCTs demonstrating that *H. pylori* eradication therapy significantly reduces the incidence of gastric cancer (RR ~0.54). Ford et al. provide the strongest interventional evidence to date that treating *H. pylori* infection prevents gastric cancer, establishing the evidence base for population-level screen-and-treat programs, particularly in high-incidence regions.
