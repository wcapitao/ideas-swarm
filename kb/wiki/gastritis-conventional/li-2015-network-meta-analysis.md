## TL;DR

Large-scale network meta-analysis comparing 14 *H. pylori* eradication regimens head-to-head. Li et al. found that concomitant quadruple therapy, hybrid therapy, and sequential therapy outperformed standard triple therapy, providing quantitative rankings that informed subsequent guideline updates on first-line and rescue regimen selection.
