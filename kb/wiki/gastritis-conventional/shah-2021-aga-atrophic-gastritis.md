## TL;DR

AGA expert review providing practical guidance on diagnosing and managing atrophic gastritis — including serological screening with pepsinogen I/II ratio and gastrin-17, the role of OLGA/OLGIM staging systems for risk stratification, and endoscopic surveillance recommendations. Bridges the gap between European consensus (MAPS II) and US clinical practice.
