## TL;DR

The CLASS trial compared celecoxib (a COX-2 selective inhibitor) with ibuprofen and diclofenac for GI safety. The initial 6-month publication reported fewer symptomatic ulcers and ulcer complications with celecoxib, though full dataset analysis later showed the advantage was less robust than initially presented. A landmark trial in the COX-2 inhibitor story and the broader NSAID gastropathy debate.
