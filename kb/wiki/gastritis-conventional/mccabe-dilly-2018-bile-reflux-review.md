## TL;DR

Clinical review of bile reflux gastropathy covering pathophysiology (detergent effect of bile acids on mucosa), etiologies (post-surgical, functional pyloric incompetence), diagnostic approaches, and treatment options including prokinetics, sucralfate, and ursodeoxycholic acid. Highlights bile reflux as an underrecognized cause of persistent gastric symptoms, especially post-cholecystectomy and post-gastric surgery.
