## TL;DR

The 2017 American College of Gastroenterology guideline provides evidence-based recommendations for *H. pylori* diagnosis and treatment. It emphasizes the declining efficacy of clarithromycin triple therapy due to rising resistance, advocates for bismuth quadruple therapy in high-resistance areas, and introduces the concept of antibiotic susceptibility-guided treatment selection.
