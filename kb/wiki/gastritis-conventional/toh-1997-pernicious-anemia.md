## TL;DR

Landmark NEJM review establishing the modern understanding of pernicious anemia as an autoimmune disease targeting gastric parietal cells and intrinsic factor. Toh et al. detail the autoantibody-mediated destruction of acid-secreting mucosa leading to achlorhydria, B12 malabsorption, and megaloblastic anemia — connecting autoimmune gastritis to its most clinically significant consequence.
