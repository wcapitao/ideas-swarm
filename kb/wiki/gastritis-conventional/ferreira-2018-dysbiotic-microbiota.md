## TL;DR

Study demonstrating that gastric cancer harbors a distinct dysbiotic microbiota with decreased diversity and enrichment of specific taxa (including oral-origin bacteria) compared to chronic gastritis. Ferreira et al. provide evidence that the gastric microbiome shifts along the carcinogenesis cascade, suggesting microbial community changes may contribute to — not merely accompany — gastric cancer development.
