## TL;DR

Correa and Piazuelo update the original 1988 gastric precancerous cascade model with two decades of molecular and epidemiological evidence. They refine the stepwise progression from normal mucosa through chronic gastritis, atrophic gastritis, intestinal metaplasia (complete and incomplete subtypes), dysplasia, and carcinoma — discussing molecular drivers at each stage and whether a "point of no return" exists beyond which *H. pylori* eradication no longer prevents cancer.
