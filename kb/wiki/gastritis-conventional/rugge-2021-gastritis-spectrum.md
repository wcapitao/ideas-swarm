## TL;DR

Contemporary review mapping the full clinico-pathological spectrum of gastritis — from *H. pylori*-associated and autoimmune forms through chemical, lymphocytic, eosinophilic, and granulomatous gastritis. Rugge et al. advocate for consistent use of the OLGA staging system to translate histological findings into clinically actionable cancer risk categories.
