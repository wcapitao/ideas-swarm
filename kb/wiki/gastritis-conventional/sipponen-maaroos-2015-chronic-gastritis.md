## TL;DR

Comprehensive review of chronic gastritis covering epidemiology, etiology (*H. pylori*, autoimmune, chemical), topographic patterns (antral-predominant vs corpus-predominant vs pangastritis), natural history, and clinical significance. Sipponen and Maaroos emphasize that the topographic pattern of gastritis determines clinical outcome — antral gastritis predisposes to duodenal ulcer while corpus/pangastritis predisposes to gastric cancer.
