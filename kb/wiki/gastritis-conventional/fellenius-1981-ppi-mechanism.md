## TL;DR

Discovery paper establishing that substituted benzimidazoles (the precursors to omeprazole and all PPIs) inhibit gastric acid secretion by specifically blocking the gastric H+/K+ ATPase (proton pump). This mechanism-of-action discovery enabled the development of the most potent class of acid-suppressing drugs, which became the backbone of gastritis and ulcer treatment.
