## TL;DR

The Maastricht V/Florence Consensus Report is the European benchmark guideline for *H. pylori* management. It broadens eradication indications, strongly recommends susceptibility-guided therapy, and positions *H. pylori* eradication as a primary gastric cancer prevention strategy. The consensus reflects the shift from treating ulcers to preventing malignancy.
