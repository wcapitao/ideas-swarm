## TL;DR

ACG guideline for preventing NSAID-related ulcer complications, establishing risk stratification (GI and cardiovascular) and recommending COX-2 selective agents plus PPI co-therapy for high-risk patients. The guideline codifies the paradigm that NSAID prescribing must balance anti-inflammatory benefit against both GI and cardiovascular harm.
