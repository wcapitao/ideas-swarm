## TL;DR

The 1994 IARC monograph classified *Helicobacter pylori* as a Group 1 definite carcinogen — only a decade after the bacterium's discovery. This classification, based on epidemiological evidence linking chronic *H. pylori* infection to gastric adenocarcinoma and MALT lymphoma, transformed *H. pylori* from an ulcer pathogen into a recognized cancer-causing agent and provided the public health rationale for population-level eradication strategies.
