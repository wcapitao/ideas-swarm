## TL;DR

The VIGOR trial demonstrated that rofecoxib (Vioxx) halved the rate of serious GI events compared to naproxen in rheumatoid arthritis patients. However, it also revealed a significant increase in cardiovascular events with rofecoxib — a signal that ultimately led to the drug's withdrawal in 2004 and reshaped the entire COX-2 inhibitor field, establishing that GI safety cannot be evaluated in isolation from cardiovascular risk.
