## TL;DR

Nature Reviews Disease Primer providing the definitive contemporary overview of autoimmune gastritis — from genetic susceptibility and immunopathogenesis through clinical presentation (iron deficiency often preceding B12 deficiency), diagnostic workup, cancer/carcinoid risk stratification, and long-term management. Establishes autoimmune gastritis as more prevalent and clinically significant than previously recognized.
