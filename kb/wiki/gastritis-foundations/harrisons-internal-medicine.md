## TL;DR

Harrison's Principles of Internal Medicine is the most widely used internal medicine textbook globally. Its gastritis and gastropathy chapters represent the current consensus on diagnosis, classification, and management — serving as the baseline "standard of care" against which any alternative or integrative approach must be compared. For a cross-paradigm KB, Harrison's defines the conventional medicine reference point.
