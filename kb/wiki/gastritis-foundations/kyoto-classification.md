## TL;DR

The 2015 Kyoto Global Consensus reclassified H. pylori gastritis as an infectious disease warranting eradication treatment regardless of symptoms — a paradigm shift from treating gastritis only when complicated by ulcers or cancer. This consensus positioned gastritis itself as a treatable condition and H. pylori eradication as the default recommendation, marking the culmination of the post-Marshall revolution. It stands in deliberate tension with Blaser's concerns about indiscriminate eradication.
