## TL;DR

Zhang Zhongjing's Shanghan Lun (~200 CE) is the foundational clinical text of Chinese herbal medicine, establishing the formula-based treatment approach still used in TCM today. Its Ban Xia Xie Xin Tang (Pinellia Decoction to Drain the Epigastrium) is one of the most studied classical formulas for gastric disorders and remains a frontline TCM treatment for functional dyspepsia and gastritis. The text's systematic matching of symptom patterns to specific herbal formulas represents the earliest protocol-driven approach to gastric disease treatment.
