## TL;DR

Samuel Fenwick's 1866 lectures established the first clinical link between chronic atrophic gastritis and pernicious anemia — a connection that would take another century to fully explain through autoimmune mechanisms. By documenting progressive gastric gland destruction and its systemic consequences, Fenwick identified one of the earliest known autoimmune disease pathways, decades before autoimmunity was conceptualized as a phenomenon.
