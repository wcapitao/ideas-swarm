## TL;DR

The Hippocratic Corpus (~400 BCE) contains the earliest systematic Western medical writings on stomach ailments. "On Regimen" and "On Affections" describe gastric disorders in terms of bile imbalance and prescribe dietary management — establishing the paradigm that stomach disease is fundamentally a disorder of diet and humoral equilibrium, a framework that persisted for two millennia.
