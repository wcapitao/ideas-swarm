## TL;DR

The Charaka Samhita (~200 BCE to 200 CE) is the foundational Ayurvedic medical text and contains the earliest known description of amlapitta — a condition closely paralleling acid gastritis. It frames gastric inflammation as a disorder of excess pitta (the fire/acid dosha) and prescribes cooling herbs, dietary modification, and lifestyle changes. This represents a complete independent medical tradition's approach to the same disease entity that Western medicine would not systematically describe for another 1,800 years.
