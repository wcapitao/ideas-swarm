## TL;DR

Robbins Pathologic Basis of Disease is the standard pathology textbook and provides the histopathological foundation for understanding gastritis at the cellular level. Its GI chapter defines what atrophy, intestinal metaplasia, dysplasia, and inflammatory infiltrates actually look like under the microscope — the ground truth behind classification systems like Sydney and OLGA. Essential reference for understanding the tissue-level evidence that underpins gastritis diagnosis and cancer risk staging.
