## TL;DR

Japanese Society of Gastroenterology clinical guidelines for functional dyspepsia that officially recommend Kampo medicine (specifically Rikkunshito) as a treatment option. Represents the rare case of a traditional medicine system being formally integrated into national clinical practice guidelines with graded evidence recommendations.

## Key concepts

- **Guideline-level Kampo recommendation** — Official inclusion of Kampo formulas in evidence-based clinical practice guidelines.
- **Japanese FD treatment algorithm** — Structured decision tree placing Kampo alongside PPIs and prokinetics as first-line options.

## Why it matters for ai-ideator

Demonstrates the endpoint of evidence accumulation: a traditional medicine formula achieving guideline-level status. Provides a model for how other traditional systems might achieve similar integration. Builds comprehensive gastritis understanding across paradigms.
