## TL;DR

Investigates the classical TCM formula Ban Xia Xie Xin Tang (Pinellia Decoction for Draining the Heart) against drug-resistant *Helicobacter pylori* strains in an animal model. Demonstrates that this formula retains efficacy where conventional antibiotic regimens fail, suggesting TCM formulas as candidates for antibiotic-resistant H. pylori eradication.

## Key concepts

- **Ban Xia Xie Xin Tang** — Classical TCM formula combining Pinellia, Scutellaria, Coptis, and other herbs; used for epigastric distress with mixed cold-heat patterns.
- **Drug-resistant H. pylori** — Strains of *H. pylori* resistant to standard triple/quadruple therapy antibiotics.

## Why it matters for ai-ideator

Demonstrates that traditional formulas designed for pattern-based diagnosis can address modern resistance problems, opening combinatorial space between TCM pattern theory and antibiotic resistance mechanisms. Builds comprehensive gastritis understanding across paradigms.
