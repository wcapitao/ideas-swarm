## TL;DR

Reports a randomized controlled trial of the Qirui Weishu capsule for chronic atrophic gastritis, providing higher-tier clinical evidence for a proprietary TCM formula. Demonstrates improvements in histological outcomes and symptom scores compared to control, representing one of the stronger RCT-level evidence points for TCM in gastritis.

## Key concepts

- **Qirui Weishu capsule** — Proprietary TCM formulation tested in a formal RCT design for chronic atrophic gastritis.
- **TCM RCT evidence** — Randomized controlled trials applying Western evidence standards to traditional formulas.
