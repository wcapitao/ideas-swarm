## TL;DR

Surveys TCM interventions targeting the progression from chronic atrophic gastritis (CAG) to gastric cancer along the Correa cascade. Reviews herbal formulas and compounds that may arrest or reverse precancerous lesions including intestinal metaplasia and dysplasia, positioning TCM as a chemoprevention strategy.

## Key concepts

- **CAG-to-cancer progression** — The Correa cascade from chronic gastritis through atrophy, intestinal metaplasia, dysplasia, to adenocarcinoma.
- **TCM chemoprevention** — Using traditional formulas to halt or reverse precancerous gastric changes.
