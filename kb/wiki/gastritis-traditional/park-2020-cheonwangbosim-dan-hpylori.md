## TL;DR

Investigates Cheonwangbosim-dan, a Korean traditional multi-herb formula, for anti-*H. pylori* activity in an animal model. Demonstrates both direct antibacterial effects and host-mediated anti-inflammatory responses, validating a Korean traditional formula originally prescribed for heart-related conditions in a gastric infection context.

## Key concepts

- **Cheonwangbosim-dan** — Korean traditional formula traditionally used for heart-nourishing purposes, repurposed for gastric anti-H. pylori investigation.
- **Formula repurposing** — Testing a traditional formula outside its classical indication based on shared pharmacological mechanisms.

## Why it matters for ai-ideator

Illustrates the concept of cross-indication discovery within traditional medicine, where a formula's pharmacological profile may be broader than its traditional indication suggests. Builds comprehensive gastritis understanding across paradigms.
