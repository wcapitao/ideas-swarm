## TL;DR

Compares the metabolomic profiles of electroacupuncture and moxibustion treatments in an animal model of atrophic gastritis. Reveals distinct metabolic pathway signatures for each modality despite similar clinical endpoints, suggesting different mechanisms of action for needle-based versus heat-based acupoint stimulation.

## Key concepts

- **Moxibustion** — Thermal stimulation of acupoints using burning Artemisia (moxa), a distinct TCM modality from needle acupuncture.
- **Differential metabolomic signatures** — Different acupoint stimulation methods produce overlapping but distinct metabolic responses.

## Why it matters for ai-ideator

Demonstrates that even within TCM acupoint therapy, modality choice matters at the molecular level, adding another dimension to the combinatorial design space. Builds comprehensive gastritis understanding across paradigms.
