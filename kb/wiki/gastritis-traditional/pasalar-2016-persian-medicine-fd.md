## TL;DR

Reviews the Persian/Unani medicine approach to functional dyspepsia, framing it through the lens of temperament (mizaj) imbalance. Describes the diagnostic framework of sue-e-mizaj (intemperament) of the stomach and the corresponding therapeutic strategies using dietary modification, lifestyle changes, and herbal compounds.

## Key concepts

- **Mizaj (temperament)** — The fundamental diagnostic category in Persian/Unani medicine; each organ has an ideal temperament that can become imbalanced.
- **Sue-e-mizaj (intemperament)** — Deviation from the stomach's ideal temperament, classified as hot, cold, wet, or dry; the Unani equivalent of gastritis classification.
