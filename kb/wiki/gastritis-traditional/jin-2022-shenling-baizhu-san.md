## TL;DR

Reviews the TCM formula Shen-Ling-Bai-Zhu-San (Ginseng, Poria, and Atractylodes Powder) for chronic gastritis, focusing on its role in strengthening spleen qi and resolving dampness. Summarizes pharmacological evidence and clinical applications for this foundational spleen-tonifying formula.

## Key concepts

- **Shen-Ling-Bai-Zhu-San** — Classical spleen-tonifying formula used for digestive weakness with diarrhea and poor appetite.
- **Spleen-qi deficiency pattern** — TCM syndrome characterized by fatigue, poor digestion, and loose stools; a common pattern in chronic gastritis.

## Why it matters for ai-ideator

Represents the spleen-qi deficiency paradigm in TCM gastritis treatment, complementing the damp-heat and cold-heat mixed paradigms covered by other formulas. Builds comprehensive gastritis understanding across paradigms.
