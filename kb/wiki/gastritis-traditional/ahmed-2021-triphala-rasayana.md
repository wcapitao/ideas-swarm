## TL;DR

Comprehensive review of Triphala, the iconic Ayurvedic three-fruit Rasayana (Amla, Haritaki, Bibhitaki), covering its pharmacology, clinical evidence, and traditional applications. Details its gastroprotective, anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and prebiotic properties relevant to gastritis and broader GI health.

## Key concepts

- **Triphala** — Three-fruit Ayurvedic Rasayana combining Amla, Haritaki (Terminalia chebula), and Bibhitaki (Terminalia bellirica); one of the most widely used Ayurvedic formulas.
- **Rasayana** — Ayurvedic category of rejuvenation therapies aimed at systemic health restoration and longevity.
- **Polyherbal synergy** — The three fruits produce effects greater than any single component, a core Ayurvedic formulation principle.
