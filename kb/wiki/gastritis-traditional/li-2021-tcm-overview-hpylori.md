## TL;DR

Comprehensive overview of Traditional Chinese Medicine approaches to *H. pylori*-associated gastritis, covering pattern differentiation strategies, commonly used formulas, and the evidence base for TCM as adjunctive or standalone therapy. Synthesizes the rationale for TCM intervention across multiple syndrome types.

## Key concepts

- **TCM pattern differentiation for gastritis** — Classifying gastritis into syndrome types (e.g., spleen-stomach damp-heat, liver-stomach disharmony) to guide formula selection.
- **Integrative H. pylori treatment** — Combining TCM formulas with or as alternatives to standard eradication therapy.
