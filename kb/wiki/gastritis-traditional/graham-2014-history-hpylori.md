## TL;DR

Historical review of the discovery of *H. pylori* and its role in gastric diseases, tracing the paradigm shift from the stress-and-acid model to the infectious etiology model. Contextualizes the Marshall-Warren discovery within the broader history of gastric medicine, including earlier observations that were overlooked or dismissed.

## Key concepts

- **H. pylori paradigm shift** — The transformation of gastric disease understanding from stress/acid to infectious etiology.
- **Gastric disease historiography** — Historical documentation of how scientific understanding of gastritis evolved over centuries.

## Why it matters for ai-ideator

Provides the historical context for understanding why traditional medicine approaches to gastritis were marginalized during the acid-suppression era and why the H. pylori revolution reopened the door to multi-causal thinking. Builds comprehensive gastritis understanding across paradigms.
