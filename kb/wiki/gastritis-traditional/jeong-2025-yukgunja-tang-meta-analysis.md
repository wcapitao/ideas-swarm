## TL;DR

Meta-analysis of Yukgunja-tang (the Korean name for Liu Jun Zi Tang / Rikkunshito) for chronic atrophic gastritis. Pools data across multiple clinical trials to provide the highest-tier evidence assessment of this widely used East Asian formula, evaluating efficacy on histological and symptomatic endpoints.

## Key concepts

- **Yukgunja-tang** — Korean name for the six-gentleman decoction (Liu Jun Zi Tang / Rikkunshito), demonstrating the pan-East-Asian use of this formula.
- **Meta-analysis of traditional formula** — Systematic pooling of clinical trial data for a traditional medicine intervention.

## Why it matters for ai-ideator

Provides meta-analytic evidence for the same formula known under three names across TCM, Kampo, and Korean medicine, illustrating cross-cultural convergence on a single therapeutic approach. Builds comprehensive gastritis understanding across paradigms.
