## TL;DR

Investigates a modified version of the Zuojin Pill and its effects on glycolysis pathways in gastric tissue. Demonstrates that the formula modulates metabolic reprogramming in damaged gastric mucosa, connecting traditional formula actions to cellular energy metabolism and mucosal repair mechanisms.

## Key concepts

- **Modified Zuojin Pill** — Augmented version of the classical two-herb formula with additional components targeting metabolic pathways.
- **Glycolysis modulation** — Regulation of cellular glucose metabolism as a mechanism of gastroprotection and tissue repair.

## Why it matters for ai-ideator

Links a classical TCM formula to metabolic reprogramming, an area of active interest in cancer biology, creating cross-paradigm bridges between traditional gastritis treatment and modern metabolic oncology. Builds comprehensive gastritis understanding across paradigms.
