## TL;DR

Applies metabolomics analysis to an animal model of chronic atrophic gastritis treated with electroacupuncture, identifying specific metabolic biomarkers and pathways altered by the intervention. Provides molecular-level evidence for electroacupuncture's gastroprotective effects beyond symptom-level outcomes.

## Key concepts

- **Electroacupuncture** — Acupuncture with electrical stimulation for standardized, reproducible dosing of needle stimulation.
- **Metabolomics profiling** — Unbiased analysis of small-molecule metabolites to characterize systemic biological responses to treatment.

## Why it matters for ai-ideator

Metabolomics provides a paradigm-neutral molecular readout of acupuncture effects, enabling direct comparison with herbal and pharmaceutical interventions at the pathway level. Builds comprehensive gastritis understanding across paradigms.
