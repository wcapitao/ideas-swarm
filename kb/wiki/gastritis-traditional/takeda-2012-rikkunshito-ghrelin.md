## TL;DR

Demonstrates that Rikkunshito acts as a ghrelin enhancer through in-vitro mechanisms, identifying specific flavonoids that potentiate ghrelin signaling via the GHS-R1a receptor. Provides the molecular basis for Rikkunshito's appetite-stimulating and prokinetic effects in functional dyspepsia.

## Key concepts

- **Ghrelin enhancement** — Rikkunshito increases circulating acyl-ghrelin levels and potentiates ghrelin receptor signaling.
- **GHS-R1a receptor** — Growth hormone secretagogue receptor targeted by ghrelin; Rikkunshito flavonoids act as positive allosteric modulators.
