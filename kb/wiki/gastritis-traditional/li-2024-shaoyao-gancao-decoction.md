## TL;DR

Examines Shaoyao Gancao Decoction (Peony and Licorice Decoction) in an animal model of chronic atrophic gastritis. This classical antispasmodic formula demonstrates gastroprotective effects through anti-inflammatory and smooth-muscle-relaxant mechanisms, validating its traditional use for abdominal cramping and pain.

## Key concepts

- **Shaoyao Gancao Decoction** — Two-herb formula (Paeonia lactiflora + Glycyrrhiza) classically used for abdominal spasm and pain; one of the simplest effective TCM formulas.
- **Peony-Licorice synergy** — Paeoniflorin (antispasmodic) combined with glycyrrhizin (anti-inflammatory) for dual-mechanism gastroprotection.

## Why it matters for ai-ideator

As a minimal two-herb formula with well-characterized pharmacology, it serves as a tractable model for understanding TCM synergy principles computationally. Builds comprehensive gastritis understanding across paradigms.
