## TL;DR

Reports a clinical pilot study of an Ayurvedic formulation (Amlapitta Mishran) for the treatment of Amlapitta, the Ayurvedic disease entity corresponding to acid gastritis. Demonstrates symptom improvement in a case series, providing preliminary clinical evidence for the Ayurvedic approach to hyperacidity and gastric inflammation.

## Key concepts

- **Amlapitta** — Ayurvedic disease entity characterized by sour/acid eructation, heartburn, and gastric inflammation; corresponds to acid gastritis/GERD.
- **Pitta-predominant gastric disorder** — Ayurvedic framing of gastritis as an excess of the pitta dosha (fire/transformation principle).
