## TL;DR

Tests mastic gum components against drug-resistant strains of *H. pylori* in vitro, including clarithromycin-resistant isolates. Demonstrates that mastic gum retains antibacterial activity against resistant strains, paralleling the Ban Xia Xie Xin Tang findings from TCM and suggesting natural products as alternatives when standard antibiotics fail.

## Key concepts

- **Drug-resistant H. pylori in vitro** — Testing natural products against clinically isolated antibiotic-resistant H. pylori strains.
- **Clarithromycin resistance** — Major clinical challenge in H. pylori eradication; mastic gum shows activity where this first-line antibiotic fails.
