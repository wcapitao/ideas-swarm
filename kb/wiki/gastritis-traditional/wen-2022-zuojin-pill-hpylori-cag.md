## TL;DR

Examines the classical two-herb formula Zuojin Pill (Coptis chinensis + Evodia rutaecarpa) in an animal model of *H. pylori*-associated chronic atrophic gastritis. Demonstrates anti-inflammatory and gastroprotective effects, supporting the traditional indication of this formula for acid reflux and epigastric pain.

## Key concepts

- **Zuojin Pill** — Minimalist TCM formula (Coptis 6:1 Evodia) for liver-fire invading the stomach, manifesting as acid reflux and bitter taste.
- **Coptis-Evodia synergy** — Cold (Coptis) and hot (Evodia) herbs combined to address mixed pathology.

## Why it matters for ai-ideator

Exemplifies the TCM principle of combining opposing thermal natures in a single formula, a design logic with no direct Western equivalent. Builds comprehensive gastritis understanding across paradigms.
