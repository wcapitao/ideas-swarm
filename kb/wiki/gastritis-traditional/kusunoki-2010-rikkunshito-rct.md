## TL;DR

Reports a randomized controlled trial demonstrating the clinical efficacy of Rikkunshito for functional dyspepsia symptoms. Provides RCT-level evidence for this Kampo formula, showing statistically significant improvement in epigastric pain, postprandial fullness, and overall symptom scores versus control.

## Key concepts

- **Rikkunshito RCT** — Randomized trial providing high-tier evidence for the most widely prescribed Kampo digestive formula.
- **Functional dyspepsia symptom scores** — Standardized outcome measures for evaluating dyspepsia treatments.

## Why it matters for ai-ideator

One of the strongest pieces of clinical evidence for any traditional medicine gastritis intervention, establishing a high-water mark for cross-paradigm evidence comparison. Builds comprehensive gastritis understanding across paradigms.
