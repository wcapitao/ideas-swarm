## TL;DR

Screens Mexican traditional medicinal plants for in-vitro anti-*H. pylori* activity, identifying several species with significant antibacterial effects. Bridges Mesoamerican ethnobotanical knowledge with modern microbiology by testing plants traditionally used for stomach complaints against the now-known causative organism.

## Key concepts

- **Mexican anti-H. pylori ethnopharmacology** — Systematic testing of plants from Mexican folk medicine against H. pylori.
- **Mesoamerican herbal gastritis treatment** — Traditional plant-based remedies for stomach ailments from pre-Columbian and mestizo healing traditions.
