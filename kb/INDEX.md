## Conventions

- Every article has YAML frontmatter matching `SCHEMA.md`.
- Every claim cites a source — either a `raw/` file or canonical URL with retrieval date.
- Connections are bidirectional where possible.
- Contradictions are flagged in a `## Contradiction` block, not silently reconciled.
- Concept articles live in `combinatorial-creativity/`; primary-source articles live in their author's domain.
